How the Group Met

Everyone in the group met through Jamie. Jamie from his mission in Los Angeles knew Joe, and Joe introduced him to his girlfriend Grace. Jamie met Hannah on a blind date with her and since then has become her boyfriend. After the group was formed, they all started texting each other. They eventually planned the camping trip to southern Utah, and invited others to join. The story is not long, but that is the gist of it.
